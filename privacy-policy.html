Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 15, 2026

1. Who We Are

Momentum is published by Adrian Gratar (the "developer", "we", "us"). Contact: the.real.momentum.app@gmail.com.

2. What We Collect — and What We Don't

We do not collect personal information. We do not require sign-up, email, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or device identifiers.

Everything you enter into Momentum — tasks, stash notes, emotional check-ins, completed sessions, optional task notes, target dates — is stored only on your device using Apple's SwiftData (an on-device SQLite store managed by iOS).

If, in a future update, you opt in to iCloud sync, your data will replicate via your private iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit. We never see that data; only your Apple ID can read it. iCloud sync is currently disabled.

3. What Gets Sent to Third Parties

AI assistance (the "I can't start" decomposition, "this feels scary" reframe, and "AI Sort" in Stash) sends only the specific text you're acting on — a task title, an avoidance reason, or a list of stash items — to OpenAI's API for processing.

No account identifier, device identifier, location, or other context is included. OpenAI's processing of these requests is governed by OpenAI's privacy policy. We do not store these requests on any server.

If no OpenAI API key is configured on the device, all AI features fall back to a deterministic offline path; nothing leaves the device in that mode.

In-app purchases are processed by RevenueCat, Inc. RevenueCat receives a randomly-assigned customer identifier and your purchase receipt; it does not receive your name, email, or any content you enter into the app. RevenueCat's processing is governed by RevenueCat's privacy policy.

The optional Calendar feature (Premium) uses Apple's EventKit on-device. Calendar data is never transmitted off the phone.

Apple Notifications, when enabled, are scheduled locally on your device by iOS. We do not push notifications from a server.

4. Premium Subscription

Momentum offers an optional auto-renewing subscription ("Momentum Premium") that unlocks additional features: AI-enhanced task breakdowns and reframing, AI-sorted Stash, calendar integration, 90-day insight history, and full data export.

The free tier includes emotional check-ins, adaptive task suggestions, focus sessions, deterministic task decomposition, and 14-day insights. No features are removed from the free tier after installation.

Your subscription status is stored locally on your device and verified through your Apple ID purchase receipts. We do not maintain user accounts or subscription databases on our servers.

5. Analytics, Tracking, Ads

None. Momentum contains no analytics SDKs (no Firebase, Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude, etc.), no crash reporting, no advertising libraries, no tracking pixels. App Store does provide aggregated, anonymous install statistics to developers; we have no control over and do not enrich that data.

6. Children

Momentum is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, and the app stores no personal data, but if you are a parent and believe your child has provided information you'd like removed, deleting the app fully removes all on-device data.

7. Your Rights (Including GDPR / CCPA)

Because all data lives on your device:

  • Access: open the app and view your data directly.
  • Deletion: delete the app, or use Settings → Reset onboarding for a partial reset.
  • Portability: Premium subscribers can export all data as JSON from Settings → Export everything.
  • Withdrawal of consent: turn off iCloud sync (when available) and notifications in iOS Settings. Cancel your subscription anytime in iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

For users in the EU/UK: the lawful basis we rely on is your direct provision of the data to your own device. We are not the data controller for data that never leaves your device.

For users in California: we do not "sell" or "share" personal information as defined by the CCPA.

8. Data Retention

Local data persists until you delete it or uninstall the app. We retain nothing on our side because we collect nothing.

9. Security

On-device data is protected by iOS sandboxing and the device passcode/Face ID/Touch ID you've set. AI requests use HTTPS with TLS. We do not operate servers of our own.

10. International Transfers

AI requests, if you trigger them, are processed by OpenAI in jurisdictions disclosed in OpenAI's policy.

11. Changes to This Policy

Material changes will be noted in the app's release notes. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Continued use after an update means you accept the updated policy.

12. Contact
